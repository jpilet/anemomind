Advanced race analytics

Anemomind and Astra Yacht combined their unique experiences to develop an advanced sailing data processing platform.

At Anemomind and at Astra Yacht, we believe technology and artificial intelligence should help sailors sail faster and have more fun. We joined our forces to create Regatta Polar, a web platform dedicated to processing, analyzing and visualizing sailing logs.

Combining Anemomind's data processing pipeline with Astra's polar generation algorithm makes it possible to process easily and automatically your sailing logs.
